House of Ano was not conceived as a business first. It emerged as a response. A response to culture being mined for trends instead of preserved as knowledge.

Founded by Oceans and Andile Ndunge, the institution rejects the idea of chasing virality. Instead, it builds structures that outlive their creators.

We function as a cultural system-builder. Our mandate is simple: Culture is not decoration. Culture is infrastructure.

The House operates under strict governance. It does not seek to dominate culture. It seeks to architect it quietly, allowing others to feel the impact without always seeing the hand.

INT. HOUSE OF ANO - DAY The room is quiet. A projector hums in the background. On the table sits the PRODUCTION MANIFESTO. It is not a menu. It is a system. HOUSE OF ANO Production is not a department. It is a discipline.
We do not separate thinking from making.
We turn meaning into form. The camera pans down the list of CAPABILITIES:
1. CREATIVE DIRECTION & ARCHITECTURE
Concept development. Narrative systems. World-building. We design the system before we design the output.
2. FILM & MOTION PRODUCTION
Short films. Brand documentaries. Visual essays. Cinematic work that prioritises atmosphere and longevity.
3. DIGITAL CONTENT & PLATFORMS
Social-first visual systems. Content ecosystems. Assets designed to live, move, and age well online.
4. 3D, CGI & ADVANCED VISUALS
Virtual prototypes. Digital scenography. Visualising ideas and futures that do not yet physically exist.
5. PHOTOGRAPHY AS DOCUMENTATION
Campaigns. Editorials. Archival imagery. We approach photography as cultural proof, not simple capture.
6. EXPERIENTIAL PRODUCTION
Spatial storytelling. Stage design. Lighting and audio-visual flow. Events designed as living systems.
7. SPATIAL & SET DESIGN
Installations. Retail environments. Pop-up interventions. Space communicates before language.
8. SOUND & AUDIO INFRASTRUCTURE
Original composition. Sonic branding. Sound design. Treating audio as an emotional layer, not an afterthought.
9. POST-PRODUCTION & FINISHING
Editing. Colour grading. Asset management. Ensuring durability and adaptability over time.
10. CULTURAL PROTOTYPING
Experimental research. Future-facing concept testing. Innovation that feeds all other layers.
